📋 How to Connect Buffer (Recommended — Free)
Buffer's RSS integration automatically posts new blog articles to your social channels:
  1. Go to buffer.com → Integrations → RSS
  2. Paste your RSS feed URL: https://akadigital.net/feed.xml
  3. Select which social channels to publish to (LinkedIn, Facebook, etc.)
  4. Set the schedule — e.g. "post immediately when new item appears"
  5. Save. Every new blog post will auto-post to your social channels.
